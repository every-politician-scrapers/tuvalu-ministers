Current Government

The Current Government is the party with the majority of votes with the number of parliament members up to twelve in total. The country is lead by the Head of State, which is also the Governor General which reflects the British Commonwealth Standards. The Tuvalu Government is lead by the Prime Minister with his cabinet Ministers.
